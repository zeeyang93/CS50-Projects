1. King's Gambit

Description

The aim of the gambit is to offer a white pawn on the f-file, to divert the black's pawn away from the center.

If the gambit is accepted, one of the most popular line is to play pawn d4 and Bxf4, regaining the gambit pawn with central domination.

This allows rapid development for white while maintaining central board control.

Pros Cons
Very exciting opening Black has many different options to choose from
White goes for the initiative Black can decline the gambit in a number of ways
Unusual positional themes White's king can become exposed
